About ChargeRight Pro

Helping EV owners install confidently and charge smarter.

Hi, I'm Vamsi Krishna (@gvamsikrishna31 on X), a tech enthusiast from India who is passionate about electric vehicles and sustainable energy. I spend my days reading spec sheets, comparing charger features, and breaking down the “what actually matters” details so EV owners can make smart decisions without getting overwhelmed.

I built ChargeRightPro.com to help EV owners save thousands on professional installs by sharing free, step-by-step guides based on real research and user experiences. My background is in tech and engineering, so I enjoy translating complex wiring, load calculations, and smart charger settings into clear, repeatable steps. EVs excite me because they blend clean energy with software-driven innovation, and the charging ecosystem is evolving faster than most people realize.

I've personally installed 12 home chargers and helped troubleshoot 60+ issues, from breaker trips and load balancing to Wi-Fi pairing and firmware updates. Those hands-on installs and user support conversations shape every guide you see here—no fluff, just what works in real garages.

When I research a topic, I cross-check manufacturer manuals, electrician forums, and real owner feedback to identify what tends to go wrong (and how to prevent it). I also keep an eye on incentive programs and utility rebates so you can take advantage of savings that many installers never mention. The goal is to make every guide feel like a trusted checklist you can follow with confidence.

ChargeRight Pro is built for everyday EV owners: first-time installers, DIYers, and anyone who wants to understand their home charging setup before hiring a pro. If you're comparing chargers, planning electrical upgrades, or troubleshooting a confusing error code, I want this site to be the most practical stop on your journey.

ChargeRight Pro is funded through affiliate partnerships, which keep the guides free and allow me to invest in testing and research. If you ever want to understand how that works, you can read the full details on the Disclosure page.

Questions? Reach me on X @gvamsikrishna31 or via hello@chargerightpro.com.
