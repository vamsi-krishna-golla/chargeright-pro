Affiliate Disclosure

How ChargeRight Pro earns revenue and maintains editorial independence

Summary

ChargeRight Pro participates in affiliate programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. When you click our links and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. This helps us keep our content free and maintain the site.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track when a visitor from our site makes a purchase on a partner website. When you click a product link on ChargeRight Pro and complete a purchase, we receive a small commission from the retailer.

This commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget—it does not increase the price you pay. You pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer's website.

Our Affiliate Partners

ChargeRight Pro currently participates in the following affiliate programs:

  • Amazon Associates: We link to EV chargers, electrical supplies, and related products available on Amazon.com

How We Choose Products to Recommend

Our editorial recommendations are based on:

  • Product quality and reliability
  • User reviews and feedback
  • Value for money
  • Brand reputation and customer support
  • Compatibility with common EV models

We only recommend products we believe will genuinely help our readers. We do not accept payment to recommend specific products, and affiliate commissions do not influence our editorial content.

Editorial Independence

Our content is created independently of our affiliate partnerships. Product recommendations are based on merit, not commission rates. If a product isn't good, we won't recommend it regardless of potential affiliate earnings.

We clearly label affiliate links and sponsored content throughout our site. Look for phrases like "affiliate link," "we may earn a commission," or "sponsored" to identify commercial relationships.

Your Trust Matters

We understand that trust is essential. Our goal is to provide genuinely helpful information that helps you make informed decisions about EV charging. Affiliate revenue allows us to maintain free access to all our guides while continuing to create new content.

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or feel that any content has been improperly influenced, please contact us.

FTC Compliance

This disclosure is provided in accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials. We are committed to transparent communication about our commercial relationships.
