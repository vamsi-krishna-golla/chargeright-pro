Quick Start Checklist

Before You Install

  • Check your electrical panel capacity (100A or 200A service)
  • Verify available breaker slots for a 50-60A double-pole breaker
  • Measure distance from panel to charging location
  • Research local permit requirements
  • Decide: DIY installation or hire an electrician
  • Choose between hardwired or plug-in (NEMA 14-50) installation
