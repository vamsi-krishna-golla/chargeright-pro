Quick Diagnostics

Before You Troubleshoot

  • Power cycle: Turn off breaker for 30 seconds, then back on
  • Reconnect: Unplug from vehicle, wait 10 seconds, replug firmly
  • Check vehicle: Ensure car is unlocked and in "Park"
  • Inspect connector: Look for debris, moisture, or damaged pins
  • Check app: Look for error messages or status updates
